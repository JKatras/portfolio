About EatIn

  • Under The Hood

  • EatIn uses the Yummly® API to find recipes based on your search guidelines. Just specify a keyword, along with whichever ingredients you would like (or not like) included in your dish and the search will handle the rest.

  • The application was developed using AngularJS, with Angular-UI/UI-Router plugin. The visual framework is Twitter Bootstrap, with back-end support from FireBase and AngularFire.
